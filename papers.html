    The Plan

    The plan I have is to document every step of the research I conduct on Adaptive Learning Systems. This will be a sequenced process, as I hope to gain enough knowledge and skills to eventually design my own ALS for my doctoral proposal.

    How will it work

    I have identified six distinct areas of study that represent the grand scope of ALS, these are; learning theories, online learning, adaptive learning design, design-based research, learning analytics and ALS work already done. Under each area of study, I will engage with and review at least five journal articles, two books and two lectures before developing my own papers on the topic. These reviews will form part of my research synthesis matrix and feed into the work I will be putting together for the development of a new ALS.

    Timelines

    I will be applying for a PhD programme position in the 2022 academic year. This means that I aim to have a basis for the development of the new ALS by the end of November 2021.
