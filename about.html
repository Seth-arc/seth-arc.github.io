      • who, what & why

    Hello there! My name is Sethu Nguna. I am a South African based instructional designer with almost a decade of experience within the Higher Education sector across different roles. My research interests are in instructional and learner design, adaptive learning and various other educational technology topics.

    As a self-taught intermediate coder, I love everything tech and long walks on the beach.

    I created this website in order to chronicle my journey in researching, designing and developing a distinctly African adaptive learning system. As a Masters candidate within Education, everything laid out across the site will form part of my application for a position in a doctorate programe. Through this site, I will document my own papers on various topics related to adaptive learning systems, reviews on relevant research as well as my own personal development work on a contextually relevant and new ALS.
